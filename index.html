How to survive in the forest 🌳

Introduction

This be a guide that will teach ye how t' survive in th' forest without any tools.

Ye will learn how t' find water, grub and shelter. You will also learn how to build a fire and how to make a trap.

What to bring?

  • Water 💧
  • Food 🍞
  • Knife 🔪
  • Tent 🏕
  • Compass 🧭
  • Flashlight 🔦
  • Matches 🔥
  • Map 🗺

What to do?

  • Find a place to camp 🏕
  • Make a fire 🔥
  • Make a shelter 🏠
  • Find food 🍞

What not to do?

  • Don't panic 😱
  • Don't eat berries 🍇
  • Don't eat bugs 🐛
  • Don't eat snakes 🐍
  • Don't eat frogs 🐸
